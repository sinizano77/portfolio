Hypixel Skyblock Discord Bot

Using an API, search the values of in-game items (using in-game currency) of a player-controlled market and bazaar. It also calculates the revenue made from producing materials that are automatically generated while offline from the game, given a time frame. Bases calculations on current prices (and potential trends of the price). This information is handled by a bot by Discord (a gaming group messaging app) and that information is then posted to a Discord server/group chat by the bot.
